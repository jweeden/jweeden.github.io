'Spot!' Dog Breeder App

This is a site spurred on by my and my fiance's love of dogs, and the realization that there really aren't any well designed breeder sites, and to top that off, most other sites seem to never update, or are full of scams.

This site set out to address those problems and create a site that gives breeders a reliable and easy way to communicate with prospective families and update litter status'. It also full fill's a similar roll for the average user, allowing them to create a profile to receive litter updates, and also communicate with the breeders about finding their newest family member.

Currently the site is a WIP, as it became a much bigger undertaking for one person than I had origionally thought, with it evolving from a simple website, to a website with profiles and breeder verification, to an app that accomplishes all of that, but in a more convenient and packaged way that allows everything to be streamlined.

It's my hope that one day this app can really be made into something that really makes a difference. You can find the WIP, and eventually the full project on Behance.
